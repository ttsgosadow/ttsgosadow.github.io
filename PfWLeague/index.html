Rules summary

During the League each player will have a number of Assets. Each Asset will give you an ability you can use once per battle. When you play a game, each player will try to take an Asset from their opponent. The winner will take one of his oppenent's Assets!
Each Asset is worth a number of points. The player who has the highest score of all his assets combined will win the Campaign!

  • Each player has minimum 3 and maximum 6 Assets. If you have fewer than three contact the person running the League; he will get you more Assets. If you have more than 6, choose an Asset to loose.
  • Assets may be used a maximum of once per game.
  • When you play a game pick an Asset from your opponent, and then one from your own Assets. If you win or draw the game you gain control of the Asset you picked from your opponent. Each Asset that changed controller loses 1 value. Each Asset that you picked for yourself gains 2 value.
  • Your first game under a given Event is played with the Event applied.
  • E-mail, app/whatever me the results of each game, including what happend to the Assets involved. I will update the site.
  • Any questions, let me know!
